Instant License Plate Hunting

"A tool that assists authoritites to find/detect cars' license plates within shortest time. Using CCTV and Machine Learning/Deep Learning at backend, ILPH has no range limited. In other words, ILPH can detect all license plates of moving vehicles and be able to detect wanted objects with locations, timeframes and follow the vehical(s)."

Why do we create Instant License Plate Hunting?

Our application is inspired by "AMBER Alert" system and ALPR (Automated License Plate Recognition). We got an idea of combining the two technologies to optimize the time of detecting the wanted vehicles. Despite the fact, AMBER Alert serves its purposes perfectly well with 94% success rate, still, there are some constraints that can limited the success. Hence, "Instant License Plate Hunting" can help to remove all kind of contraints that existed it the previous system.
