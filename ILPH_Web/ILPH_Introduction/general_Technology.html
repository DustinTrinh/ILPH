Technology Breakdown

Step 1

Input information such as: Car brand and model, color and License Plate into the Form then add to Hunting Queue. 

Step 2

"Instant License Plate Hunting" application will connect to all CCTV available and take snapshots instantly. It will store all the images into a folder.

Step 3

ILPH will use all images taken before and start to analyze. It will start to detect cars and read the characteristics such as car's brand, model and its license plate. 

Step 4

If ILPH detects the wanted vehicles, notifications will appear and provide authorities with Location and image. It will also keeping track of the vehicle movements and update the authorities with a path.

Step 5

After the case resolved, authorities will tell ILPH to close the case, else it will keep searching for that vehicles.
