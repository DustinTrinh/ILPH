Analysis & Improvements

"Not to Replace, but to Improve"

We admire the AMBER Alert's goal and acheivements with 94% of success rate. We inherit the idea and try to improve it. Still, there are huge amounts of risks that involve in the system of using Citizen sourcing. Hence, we came up with an idea of using another aproach with the same goal, and aiming for a more effective results.

AMBER Alert's Contraints

#1

Might as well notify the wanted targets that they are being wanted. (Which sometimes can put victims in danger).

#2

Hard to manage with multiple cases. Citizens cannot keep track or remember the details with multiple cases.

#3

Might distract citizens who are driving. 

#4

Citizen sources are not always available. For example, very late at night or small streets with lack of vehicles.

#5

Cannot generate useful information if citizens are not on the street.

#6

Not real-time effective. Citizens have to make a call and authorities will take some times to react to it.

#7

Hard to keep track of the wanted vehicles which requires citizens to follow the targets in order to know the exact locations.

How ILPH can eliminate the Constraints?

Availability

The system will use CCTV and Cameras which is 24/7 available and can access anytime.

Management

Able to manage multiples reports and multiple searches at same time.

Real-time Effectiveness

ILPH can use all CCTV and look for wanted vehicles in real-time. It can also track and draw paths of the targets then notify the authorities.

Safety

By operate quietly and fast, it is a lot safer for both Victims and Drivers.
